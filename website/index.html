Fully agent-native, fully agent-driven

pftui is designed to be operated by your AI agent. Every feature has a CLI counterpart with --json output. The agent guide (AGENTS.md) covers setup, daily operation, and long-term system design, so any agent can run pftui as thoroughly as a human analyst.

One-Command Intelligence

$ pftui refresh && pftui brief --agent --json

Your agent fetches 10+ data sources and gets a complete portfolio snapshot, positions, macro, news, sentiment, predictions, movers, in one JSON blob. No web searching required.

Agent-Guided Setup

"Read AGENTS.md and set up pftui for me"

Tell Claude Code, Codex, or OpenClaw to read the agent guide. It will ask about your holdings, risk tolerance, and market views, then populate everything automatically.

Automated Daily Briefs

$ pftui eod && pftui movers --json

Set up cron jobs for morning briefs and market close summaries. Your agent monitors, analyses, and delivers, you read on your phone or terminal.

Your personal database. Your proprietary edge.

Every refresh seeds your personal market database with data from 10+ sources (SQLite or PostgreSQL). Over time, you build a proprietary data collection the kind trading desks pay six figures for, and you fully own it.

🏦

Hedge fund analytics, zero cost

Daily OHLCV history, COT positioning, COMEX inventory, sentiment indices, economic releases, prediction odds, all accumulating locally. Query it however you want. Build your own backtests, correlations, and regime models.

🔐

Full data sovereignty

No cloud sync. No third-party accounts. No data harvesting. Use a local SQLite file at ~/.local/share/pftui/pftui.db or your own PostgreSQL instance. Back it up, version it, or migrate it, your data stays yours.

📐

18 tables, one source of truth

Transactions, price history, COT data, COMEX warehouse reports, BLS economic data, World Bank global macro, sentiment indices, prediction markets, news, alerts, journal entries, all normalised, all queryable.

⚡

Compounds over time

Day one you get a snapshot. Day thirty you have a month of cross-asset history. Day three hundred you have a dataset most retail investors never build. The longer you run it, the more powerful it becomes.

Analytics Engine

The only retail tool with a multi-timeframe analytics engine. From intraday volatility to decade-long empire cycles.

Four-layer intelligence stack

LOW → MEDIUM → HIGH → MACRO

LOW (hours→days): prices, sentiment, correlations, alerts. MEDIUM (weeks→months): scenarios, thesis, conviction. HIGH (months→years): trend tracker + evidence. MACRO (years→decades): structural cycles + power metrics.

Cross-timeframe signals

$ pftui analytics signals --json

Refresh computes alignment, divergence, and transition signals across layers. Your agent gets structured signal logs for prioritization, briefing, and escalation.

Terminal demo scene

$ pftui analytics summary

LOW: risk-off │ MEDIUM: war scenario rising │ HIGH: AI displacement accelerating │ MACRO: reserve-currency decline. Alignment score and top signal in one view.
