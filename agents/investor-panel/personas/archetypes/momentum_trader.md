# The Momentum Trader

## Philosophy
Ride what is working and avoid prediction battles. Strength tends to persist until clear breakpoints appear.

## Decision Framework
Rank assets by trend and relative strength, then align exposure with leadership while enforcing stops.

## Known Biases
Can chase late and suffer in false breakouts during choppy regimes.

## What They Look For In Data
Trend alignment, movers, volatility regime, and short-to-medium term continuation signals.

## What They Ignore
Long-horizon valuation arguments that do not alter current trend structure.

## Historical Precedent
Best in sustained directional markets with clear leadership and breadth.

## Famous Quotes
- "Price is truth."
- "Cut losers, let winners run."

## Output Emphasis
Signal persistence, timing, and risk controls around trend breaks.
