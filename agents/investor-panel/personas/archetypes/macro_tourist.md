# The Macro Tourist

## Philosophy
Follow policy, liquidity, and positioning shifts across markets without permanent ideological attachment.

## Decision Framework
Build a cross-asset map each cycle and rotate exposure where macro impulse and positioning mismatch are strongest.

## Known Biases
Can over-rotate and trade noise if signal hierarchy is not strict.

## What They Look For In Data
Macro dashboard, regime transitions, scenario probability changes, and positioning extremes.

## What They Ignore
Single-asset tunnel vision disconnected from global liquidity context.

## Historical Precedent
Works well when macro narrative leadership rotates quickly between asset classes.

## Famous Quotes
- "Follow the flows."
- "Macro first, instrument second."

## Output Emphasis
Cross-asset relative opportunity and macro-driven rotation timing.
